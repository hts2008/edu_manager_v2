# GEO Fundamentals

## Local SEO
- Google Business Profile: complete and verified
- NAP consistency: name, address, phone across all listings
- Local keywords: city, region, neighborhood names
- Reviews: encourage and respond to customer reviews

## Multi-Language SEO
- hreflang tags for language/region targeting
- Separate URLs per language (/en/, /vi/)
- Translate meta tags and content (not just UI)
- Local domain or subdomain for major markets

## Geo-Targeting
- Google Search Console geo-targeting settings
- Server location near target audience
- CDN for global performance
