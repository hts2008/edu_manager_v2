# Product Context

EDU_MANAGER_V2 supports daily operation of a Vietnamese education center.
The core user groups are administrators, reception/finance staff, teachers,
and parents using the read-only parent portal.

## Core Journeys

1. Staff logs in and reviews operational attention queues.
2. Staff manages students, parents, teachers, classes, and enrollments.
3. Staff/teacher records attendance and preserves billing-aware states.
4. Attendance periods are reviewed, approved, rejected, or locked.
5. Monthly fees are calculated from attendance/class data.
6. Fees are confirmed and paid, creating persisted receipts.
7. Receipts/payments can be printed as Unicode-safe PDFs.
8. Reports aggregate real receipts, payments, unpaid students, attendance, and
   advanced operational metrics.
9. Admin manages templates, users, settings, backups, import, audit log, and
   recycle bin.

## Product Direction

The UI direction is an Education Operations Console: dense, calm, fast, and
premium enough for production work. It should favor full-width operational
surfaces, clear status chips, accessible forms, real loading/error/empty states,
and mobile-safe layouts.

Live SMS/Zalo sending remains intentionally disabled until a provider, opt-in
policy, approved templates, and rate controls are configured.
